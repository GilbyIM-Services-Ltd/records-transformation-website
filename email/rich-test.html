Summer Holiday Rollout
We have rolled out to a new school in the North West and the feedback has been brilliant.  We always aim for 100% satisfaction on these busy projects and it's great to know things went so well for a new school.

Over the summer holidays we:
  • arrange for their paper records were collected organised and scanned
  • designed, agreed and configures a fileplan, naming conventions and retention schedule
  • imported and validated the scanned records
  • set up user accounts, ready for the new term

The entire project was conducted remotely, with just one kick-off meeting and the rest by email.

Product News
Over the weekend of 16th & 17th October we carried out a major upgrade of our Micro Focus Content Manager operating platform from version 9 to version 10.  These major step upgrades are only released every 12 to 18 months, but it's important to keep on top of these changes to ensure we get all the new functionality.  In the background we also apply minor patches twice daily automatically, which keep us secure with virus definitions, etc.

Over the last month we have also introduced some great new functionality to make life easier for users:
  • Self service password reset
  • OCR images at the point of saving, so you can search the content of your image even if the aren't documents
  • Wildcard searching, handy for difficult to spell names

All of this development work is provided free to users, as part of the subscription to GilbyIM.
