IRMS Conference
 
We were delighted to be able to sponsor the Information and Records Management Society Conference in Birmingham.

Our stand looked great and the contacts we made were from a wide range of industries but all had one thing in common - a passion for managing information properly throughout its lifecycle.

GilbyIM was the runner up in the Innovation of the Year Award and given the product is new and few people in the society had heard of us, the this was a huge acknowledgement to the quality of GilbyIM.

Congratulations Thomas!
We recently attended The Education People Show and got attendees to participate in a timed competition to win a box of chocolates or a bottle of champagne.

Entrants had to:
  • Create a set of 9 nested folders
  • Record 6 items of metadata (name, staff number, job title, role type, and start and end dates)
  • Save a document to a sub folder

We were expecting for times in the region of three minutes given there was lots of noise and we were standing over a laptop, but the slowest entry was just 66 seconds which was amazing.

Product News
 
Our latest product enhancement is the addition of a preview pane so that users can see the contents of PDFs without having to download them.

This was implemented within a week of receiving the feature requests.

We're now exploring the feasibility of extending this functionality to other types of documents.

Hospital School
We are pleased to welcome a new member to the GilbyIM community.  It's a hospital school in London. It's a hospital school in London, which adds to our portfolio of users from Primary and Special schools, and a Pupil Referral Unit.

They school will be managing their HR, pupil and finance records in GilbyIM.  Our scanning partner will be digitising their pupil records according to the BS10008 standard.  This means their records are scanned to a recognised standard which allows for the destruction of the originals should they wish to.

They are due to go live in January 2022.
