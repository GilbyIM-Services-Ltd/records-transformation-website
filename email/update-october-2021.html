IRMS Conference 2021
GTM Summit Registration Now Open!
 
We are looking forward to our virtual GTM Summit 2022, which will be held 2-4 November 2021. We selected these dates specifically to ensure we start our year completely aligned and make significant progress on our Q1 goals, well ahead of the holidays.

The theme of this year’s GTM Summit is “Evolve”, which underscores the necessity to have a culture of continuous improvement. It means that we’re consistently adapting so that every day we are working smarter and better than we were the day before. During Summit, the leadership team will be sharing our priorities and plans for the upcoming fiscal year that support the Micro Focus vision. The entire GTM team and supporting functional teams will be attending, as it takes ALL of us executing on the same plan to make meaningful progress.

In addition to the traditional sales plays and product demo sessions that support you as sales and services specialists, we will have dedicated Partner sessions available. And new this year is a Partner Booth – the place to go for questions and to learn more about recent Partner Program updates, trainings and certifications. Live chat will be open to engage with Partner Program and Enablement team.

Registration is now open.
Please use the link for the region/time zone most relevant for you to complete the registration process before 25 October 2021.
