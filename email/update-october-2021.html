MDF claiming deadline (FY2021/H2)
The MDF claiming deadline for H2/21 (activities executed between May and October) is 15 December 2021. Please ensure you submit your MDF claims into the tool by the deadline.
If you have any questions, we kindly request you to contact our Micro Focus MDF team.

PO Number Requirement
As we moved to Enterprise platform for all MDF activities that are newly submitted and approved, the system will generate a PO (Purchase Order) which will be sent to the activity submitter. It is important you stamp the PO number in the tax invoice and upload that document in the MDF claiming portal while submitting your claim. Please note: Micro Focus MDF/Finance team will not accept any tax invoice copy without the PO number stamped on the invoice.

If you have not received the PO after your MDF activity is approved, then please reach out to the MDF team.

Best practices of MDF
Use the "sourced by MDF" tick box to tag your opportunity with the MDF activity you had run. This will help us to measure the Return of Investment on the MDF you received from us.

Partner Enablement Bulletin
Don’t miss out on the latest training and enablement offerings available to you! Check out the Partner Enablement Bulletin – October 2021 and expand your Micro Focus knowledge today!
Micro Focus will standardize the sale of its Education offerings through Flexible Credits effective November 1, 2021
At this same time, we will discontinue the use of Training Units and other Education SKUs. The only exception to this change is the Adoption Readiness Tool (ART) which will continue to be sold through its existing product SKUs. Starting today, you must use Flexible Credits for new Education quotes that will book 1 November 2021 or later:
  • For quotes already sent out that are scheduled to book after 1 November, you will need to regenerate/resend the quote using the Flexible Credit SKU (SU-AA065).
  • In-flight quotes with Education SKUs that will book before 1 November will be processed as usual.
Learn more about Flexible Credits here. See the customer communication for details about how your customer’s existing Training entitlements will be transitioned. Reach out to your regional Education contact for pricing assistance.
